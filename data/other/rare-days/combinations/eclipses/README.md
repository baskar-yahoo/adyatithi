## ★Chūḍāmaṇi-Chandra-Grahaṇam
_When a lunar eclipse occurs on a Monday, it is even more special: चूडामणि, i.e. crest jewel._

## ★Chūḍāmaṇi-Sūrya-Grahaṇam
