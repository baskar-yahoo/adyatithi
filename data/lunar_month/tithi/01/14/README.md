## Damanaka-Chaturdaśī
दमनक-चतुर्दशी is observed on Śukla-Caturdaśī tithi of Caitraḥ (lunar) month (sunrise (default)/puurvaviddha (default)).

_Celebrating the stealing of Damanaka leaves by Jagannath Prabhu from Indra's garden._
